Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Kuruwa respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Booking and caregiving preferences
  • Usage data such as browser type, device, and pages visited

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage caregiving services
  • Improve user experience and functionality
  • Communicate with users regarding bookings or updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also opt out of certain communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: kuruwa@helpinghand.com
